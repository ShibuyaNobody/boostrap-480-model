Jujutsu Kaisen

Yuuji Itadori spends his days in either the clubroom or the hospital, where he visits his ill grandpa, indulging in spurious paranormal activities with the Occult Club. However, when he unwittingly comes upon a cursed artifact, his peaceful existence takes a bizarre turn. After ingesting the stated object, revealed to be a finger belonging to the demon Sukuna Ryoumen, the "King of Curses," Yuuji finds himself propelled into the world of Curses—dreadful monsters produced by human hate and negativity—triggering a sequence of extraordinary events. As Yuuji finds his own unexpected abilities, he sees firsthand the danger these Curses represent to civilization. After being introduced to the Tokyo Metropolitan Jujutsu Technical High School, he begins to go down a path that he will never be able to return from: the path of a Jujutsu sorcerer.
